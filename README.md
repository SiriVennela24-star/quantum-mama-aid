<h1 align="center">QuantumMom</h1>
<h3 align="center">Intelligent Emergency Care Platform for Pregnant Women</h3>

<p align="center">
A smart maternal healthcare platform that combines machine learning, geospatial analytics, and quantum-inspired optimization to support pregnant women with health monitoring and emergency hospital guidance.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Project Overview</h2>

<p>
<b>QuantumMom</b> is a web-based maternal healthcare application designed to assist pregnant women throughout their pregnancy journey. The platform provides health insights, pregnancy risk prediction, and an emergency response system that guides users to the most suitable nearby hospital.
</p>

<p>
During critical situations, users can activate <b>Emergency Mode</b>, which sends alerts to emergency contacts and provides optimized navigation to the best available hospital.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Problem Statement</h2>

<p>
Maternal healthcare accessibility remains a major challenge in many regions. Pregnant women often face delays in reaching hospitals during emergencies due to lack of guidance, slow response systems, and difficulty identifying the right medical facility.
</p>

<p>
Most pregnancy apps focus only on reminders or educational content, but they rarely provide real-time emergency decision support.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Our Solution</h2>

<p>
QuantumMom combines healthcare insights with intelligent algorithms to provide:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Pregnancy health monitoring</li>
<li>Risk prediction using machine learning</li>
<li>Emergency contact alerts</li>
<li>Optimized hospital selection</li>
<li>Navigation to the nearest suitable hospital</li>
</ul>

<p>
This enables faster and more informed decisions during maternal emergencies.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Key Features</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>Patient Registration</b> – Collects health data such as age, blood pressure, weight, pregnancy stage, and emergency contacts.</li>
<li><b>Health Dashboard</b> – Displays baby development insights, nutrition guidance, and predicted pregnancy risk levels.</li>
<li><b>Emergency Mode</b> – One-tap activation with contraction timer and emergency alerts.</li>
<li><b>Hospital Optimization</b> – Recommends the most suitable nearby hospital.</li>
<li><b>Navigation Support</b> – Direct route guidance to the selected hospital.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Technology Stack</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>Frontend:</b> React, TypeScript</li>
<li><b>Build Tool:</b> Vite</li>
<li><b>Styling:</b> Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui</li>
<li><b>Animations:</b> Framer Motion</li>
<li><b>Routing:</b> React Router</li>
<li><b>Maps:</b> OpenStreetMap, Google Maps</li>
<li><b>Testing:</b> Vitest</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Algorithms Used</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)</b> – Predicts pregnancy health risk levels.</li>
<li><b>Haversine Formula</b> – Calculates real-world distance between the user and hospitals.</li>
<li><b>Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA)</b> – Ranks hospitals using quantum-inspired optimization.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Challenges Faced</h2>

<p>
One of the main challenges was adapting theoretical optimization algorithms such as QAOA to a practical healthcare use case. We also worked on integrating mapping systems and designing a user interface that remains simple and accessible during emergency situations.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Impact</h2>

<p>
QuantumMom aims to improve maternal healthcare accessibility by providing intelligent decision support during emergencies. By combining predictive analytics and optimized routing, the platform helps reduce delays in reaching appropriate medical care.
</p>
